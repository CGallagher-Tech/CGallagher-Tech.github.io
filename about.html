About Me

I'm Cameron Gallagher — a Computer Science student at the University of Newcastle (UON). I'm passionate about building practical software that blends clean design with strong engineering fundamentals.

My main interests sit at the intersection of Artificial Intelligence, Software Development, and Research. I enjoy working hands-on — whether that's building mobile apps, designing AI models, or experimenting in game development.

Outside of development, I'm a dedicated Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu practitioner. The discipline and problem-solving mindset that comes from competition carries directly into how I approach code — composed under pressure and always looking for the better move.

My long-term goal is to grow into an AI software developer, creating applications that make technology more useful, accessible, and genuinely human-centred.
